# Instagram Insights Viewer

A privacy-focused, client-side application to analyze your Instagram connections. Identify who doesn't follow you back, track your stats, and manage your network with a beautiful, mobile-first interface.

![Project Screenshot](https://via.placeholder.com/800x450?text=Instagram+Insights+Preview)

-   **Privacy First**: All processing happens 100% in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
-   **Not Following Back**: Instantly identify users you follow who don't follow you back.
-   **ZIP Parsing**: Simply drag and drop your full Instagram Data Export ZIP or individual JSON files.
-   **Stats Dashboard**: Visual overview of your Followers, Following, and Non-Followers counts.
-   **Rich UI**: Modern Glassmorphism design with dark mode and mobile responsiveness.
-   **Direct Actions**: One-click "Unfollow" button linking to Instagram profiles.
